# ClubLinked

ClubLinked is a modern student organization platform that helps students discover, join, and manage clubs while helping club leaders run applications, interviews, members, events, announcements, elections, onboarding, and internal operations from one centralized system.

The goal is to replace scattered workflows across Google Forms, spreadsheets, emails, group chats, link trees, calendar tools, and outdated university directories with one secure, fast, and premium platform built specifically for campus organizations.

## Product Purpose

ClubLinked exists to make student organizations easier to discover, join, and operate.

Most universities have hundreds or thousands of clubs, but the experience is fragmented. Students struggle to find the right organizations, understand recruitment timelines, track applications, and stay updated. Club leaders waste time managing forms, spreadsheets, interviews, events, messages, and member records manually.

ClubLinked solves this by becoming the operating system for student life.

## Core Users

### Students

Students use ClubLinked to discover clubs, view club profiles, save organizations, apply, track applications, attend events, receive updates, and manage their campus involvement.

### Club Admins

Club admins use ClubLinked to manage their club profile, applications, applicant pipeline, interviews, members, roles, announcements, events, elections, onboarding, and internal operations.

### University Admins

University admins can eventually use ClubLinked to manage organization approvals, compliance, event visibility, engagement analytics, officer verification, and campus wide student organization oversight.

5. Optional university single sign on
6. Role based access control
7. Encrypted data storage
8. Encrypted file uploads
9. Secure file access
10. Audit logs
11. Private internal notes
12. Data retention controls
13. Data deletion tools
14. Export permission controls
15. Admin permission controls
16. Rate limiting
17. Input validation
18. Server side authorization checks
19. Secure session management
20. Protection against unauthorized club access

Privacy principles:

1. Collect only necessary data
2. Never expose private applicant information publicly
3. Do not let general members see application data
4. Let students view what they submitted
5. Let admins control who can access sensitive information
6. Archive or delete old recruitment data responsibly
7. Keep AI outputs transparent and reviewable

## Example Student Journey

A student signs up with their school email, creates a profile, browses clubs, saves several organizations, attends information sessions, applies to clubs through ClubLinked, tracks all applications from one dashboard, schedules interviews, receives decisions, joins a club, and then follows club announcements and events through the platform.

## Example Club Admin Journey

A club president claims or creates a club profile, updates the public page, creates an application form, opens recruitment, reviews applicants in a pipeline, assigns interviewers, sends interview invites, makes decisions, accepts new members, runs onboarding, posts announcements, manages events, and maintains member records through one dashboard.
